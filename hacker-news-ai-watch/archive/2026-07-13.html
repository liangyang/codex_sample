World AI News Connection

Today's HN discussion connects model progress to the less visible systems wrapped around it. Token overhead, caching, tool schemas, permissions, provenance labels, and rollback plans are becoming first-class product and infrastructure concerns as agents move into routine work.

The practical reports also suggest a useful deployment rule: start where outputs can be compared, failures are detectable, and downside is bounded. Legacy visualization, trace replay, causal interventions, and declarative orchestration all create surfaces where claims can be tested rather than accepted from model or vendor headlines.
